Rho-9 Privacy Disclosure

Last Updated: June 24, 2024

By adding Rho-9 to your server, or using its services, you agree to the following terms outlined in this Privacy Disclosure. The Bot is operated by o5_redacted_.

1. Report Storage

Reports concerning user behavior are stored to enhance security features and monitor compliance. These reports may include user IDs, messages, and relevant context.

2. No Payment Information Storage

The Bot does not retain payment details. All financial transactions are handled through Discord and via Stripe donations, adhering to their respective privacy standards.

3. Data Security

The commitment to data security is paramount. The Bot developer implements robust measures to protect user data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

4. In-House Software Usage

The Bot utilizes proprietary in-house developed software to provide a graphical user interface (GUI) specifically designed for:

  • Viewing message history of channels with errors
  • Sending messages via the bot (Developer Messages)
  • Joining voice channels (VC)
  • Other troubleshooting functions

This interface does not display information entered into slash commands to ensure privacy.

5. Data Sharing

User data is not shared with third parties unless required by law or necessary to respond to legal requests. There is a strong commitment to not selling, leasing, or distributing user data for commercial purposes.

6. Data Retention

Data, including reports, is retained only as long as necessary to fulfill the intended security and operational purposes. Efforts are made to minimize the retention of personally identifiable information.

7. User Rights

Users have the right to inquire about their data, correct inaccuracies, or request data deletion. Such requests should be directed to o5_redacted_.

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

The Bot developer may update this Privacy Disclosure. Users will be notified of changes, and continued use of the Bot after updates signifies acceptance of the new terms.

9. Conditional Privacy Protection

Privacy protections are conditional upon proper use of the Bot. Intentional misuse, abuse, or attempts to bypass security measures will result in enhanced logging and monitoring to protect the integrity of the Bot and its users. If deemed necessary, collected data will be sent to authorities or Discord, depending on what is most appropriate. Human-assisted logging will be used, including screenshots, to ensure comprehensive security.
