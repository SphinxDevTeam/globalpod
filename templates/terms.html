Potential Blacklist Reasons

Understanding why users might be blacklisted from Rho-9

Important Notice

Being blacklisted will result in a complete loss of access to Rho-9's services and may prevent access to servers protected by Rho-9.

Discord TOS Violations

Any actions that violate Discord's Terms of Service may result in an immediate blacklist. This includes but is not limited to harassment, hate speech, and platform abuse.

Illegal Activities

Distribution of malware, unauthorized system access attempts, or engagement in any illegal activities will result in an immediate blacklist and potential reporting to authorities.

Report System Abuse

Submitting false reports or spamming the reporting system will lead to a blacklist. The reporting system is a critical security feature that must be used responsibly.

Bot Abuse

Attempts to abuse, exploit, or manipulate the bot's functionality will result in an immediate blacklist. This includes attempts to bypass security measures or exploit vulnerabilities.

Severe Content Violations

Sharing of CSAM, malware, or other harmful content will result in an immediate blacklist and reporting to appropriate authorities.

Blacklist Appeals

Blacklist decisions are typically final. However, if you believe you've been wrongfully blacklisted, you may contact us to discuss your case. Note that not all appeals will be considered.
