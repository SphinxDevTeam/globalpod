Security Features

Account Age Limit

Bot will automatically kick new accounts unless they are at least 7 days 1 minute old. This helps prevent raids and spam from newly created accounts.

Anti-Grabify Protection

We'll blacklist any grabify domain you report. We share your concern about privacy invasion and actively work to protect your community from tracking attempts.

Anti-Phishing/Scam Scanner

Rho-9 actively scans all links and compares them to our dual database. If a domain or URL matches our entries, the message will be deleted and the user will be banned to protect your community.

User Reporting System

Report users who consistently demonstrate malicious behavior or negatively impact the Discord experience for others. Help us maintain a safe environment for everyone.

Link Risk Reporting System

Report suspicious links not currently detected by our bot. Risk levels:

  • High Risk: Extremely dangerous (malware, etc.)
  • Medium Risk: Convincing scams, shady websites
  • Low Risk: Possible inconvenience, but not dangerous
  • Safe: Report false positives
